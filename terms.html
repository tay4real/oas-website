6 OkRide — Rider Terms

Verification requirements

All riders must complete OAS Ltd's verification process before accepting rides. This includes NIN verification, police background check, safety training, and vehicle inspection. Providing false information during verification is grounds for permanent ban and may result in legal action.

Rider obligations

  • Maintain a valid motorcycle licence and vehicle registration at all times
  • Only use your verified motorcycle for SafeRide trips
  • Wear a helmet and ensure passengers wear one at all times
  • Follow all Nigerian traffic laws and road safety regulations
  • Never deviate from the agreed route without passenger consent
  • Keep your vehicle in safe, roadworthy condition
  • Treat passengers with professionalism and respect at all times

Online status

Only go online when you are genuinely available to accept rides. Repeatedly accepting and cancelling rides, or going online without intent to work, may result in account suspension.

Re-verification

OAS Ltd conducts periodic re-verification every 6 months. Failure to complete re-verification within the grace period will result in automatic suspension until completed.

Riders who receive 3 or more SOS alerts linked to their trips, or whose rating drops below 3.0, will be automatically suspended pending investigation.

7 Payments & Refunds

Payment processing

All payments are processed by Paystack, a licensed Nigerian payment processor. By making a payment through SafeRide, you agree to Paystack's terms of service.

Fare calculation

Fares are calculated based on distance and the prevailing rate per kilometre. The estimated fare shown before booking may differ slightly from the final fare if the actual route differs from the estimated route.

Rider settlement

Riders receive 85% of the trip fare. OAS Ltd retains 15% as a platform commission. Rider earnings are settled automatically to their registered bank account on a T+1 basis (next business day) via Paystack.

Refunds

  • Refunds may be issued at OAS Ltd's discretion in cases of verified service failure
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of the trip via info@oassolutions.com.ng
  • Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days
  • No refunds are issued for completed trips where the service was delivered as described

Failed payments

If a payment fails, the trip will be cancelled automatically. OAS Ltd is not responsible for losses arising from payment processing failures caused by your bank or card issuer.
