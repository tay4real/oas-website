1 Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Otubusin Ademuyiwa Solutions Ltd ("OAS Ltd", "we", "us", or "our"), a technology company registered in Nigeria under the Corporate Affairs Commission with RC Number RC7765644.

Our registered address is: 4, Samuel Aderinkoye Lane, Off Abusoro Road, Ijoka, Akure, Ondo State, Nigeria.

This policy covers all OAS Ltd products and services, including but not limited to SafeRide Ondo (our verified motorcycle-taxi platform), Study Companion, School Manager, RentEasy Nigeria, and the OAS Ltd website at oassolutions.com.ng.

By using any OAS Ltd product or service, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Full name, phone number, and email address when you create an account
  • National Identification Number (NIN) — collected from riders only, hashed immediately and never stored in plain text
  • Bank account details — collected from riders for payment settlement purposes only
  • Profile photo and identity documents — collected from riders for verification purposes
  • Emergency contact names and phone numbers — provided voluntarily by passengers
  • Messages sent through our contact form on oassolutions.com.ng

Information collected automatically

  • GPS location data — collected during active trips only, with your explicit consent
  • Device information — device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage data — how you interact with our apps and which features you use
  • Trip data — pickup location, destination, distance, duration, and fare

Information from third parties

  • Payment information processed by Paystack — we do not store card details
  • Identity verification results from NIMC (National Identity Management Commission)

3 How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

To provide our services

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Matching passengers with verified riders on SafeRide Ondo
  • Processing payments and settling rider earnings
  • Providing real-time GPS trip tracking
  • Sending OTP codes for secure login

For safety and security

  • Verifying rider identity and conducting background checks
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and abuse
  • Responding to SOS emergency alerts — sharing location with emergency contacts and security agencies
  • Monitoring for route deviations during active trips

To communicate with you

  • Sending trip confirmations, receipts, and notifications via SMS
  • Responding to enquiries sent through our contact form
  • Sending important updates about our services

To improve our products

  • Analysing usage patterns to improve app performance
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Developing new features based on user behaviour

4 How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

With service providers

  • Paystack — for processing payments. Governed by Paystack's privacy policy.
  • Termii — for sending SMS notifications and OTP codes.
  • Google Maps — for routing and location services. Governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Supabase — for secure database and file storage hosting.

Between passengers and riders

  • A passenger's first name is shared with their matched rider
  • A rider's name, plate number, and rating are shared with their matched passenger
  • Real phone numbers are never shared — all communication is through masked channels

With emergency services

  • In the event of an SOS alert, your GPS location and trip details may be shared with emergency contacts you have registered and relevant security agencies

For legal compliance

  • We may disclose your information if required by Nigerian law, court order, or government authority

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5 Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement the following measures:

  • All data transmitted between our apps and servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • NIN numbers are hashed using bcrypt — the raw NIN is never stored or logged
  • Database is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Identity documents are stored in a private, access-controlled storage bucket
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised OAS Ltd personnel only
  • All administrative actions are logged in an append-only audit trail
  • JWT tokens expire and are refreshed regularly to prevent unauthorised access

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is completely immune to security risks. We encourage you to use a strong PIN for your device and to report any suspicious activity to us immediately at info@oassolutions.com.ng.

6 Your Rights

Under Nigerian data protection regulations (NDPR), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — you can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to deletion — you can request that we delete your account and personal data, subject to legal and regulatory requirements
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can withdraw consent for location tracking at any time by going offline in the rider app or closing the passenger app
  • Right to object — you can object to how we use your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@oassolutions.com.ng. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

7 Cookies

Our website oassolutions.com.ng uses minimal cookies essential for the website to function correctly. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

  • Essential cookies — required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics cookies — anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the website (Cloudflare Analytics — no personal data collected)

Our mobile apps (SafeRide Ondo and future products) do not use cookies. They use secure local storage on your device to maintain your session.

8 Children's Privacy

Our products and services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at info@oassolutions.com.ng and we will delete the information promptly.

9 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account data — retained while your account is active and for 2 years after closure
  • Trip data — retained for 3 years for regulatory and dispute resolution purposes
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years as required by Nigerian financial regulations
  • GPS location data — raw location pings are deleted after 90 days. Aggregated trip routes are retained for 1 year.
  • Identity documents — rider NIN documents retained for the duration of their active registration and 1 year after deactivation
  • OTP codes — deleted immediately after use or expiry (5 minutes)

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify registered users via SMS or in-app notification
  • Post a notice on our website oassolutions.com.ng

Your continued use of our products after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
