OkRide

A verified motorcycle-taxi booking platform for Ondo State. Every rider is NIN-verified and background-checked. Passengers get real-time GPS tracking, one-tap SOS emergency alerts, and automatic cashless payments.

  • NIN verification & police background checks for all riders
  • Real-time GPS tracking with family sharing
  • One-tap SOS with automatic emergency alerts
  • Automatic cashless settlement — riders paid daily

Why OAS Solutions Ltd

Built by Nigerians,
for Nigerians

We understand the Nigerian context because we live it. Our products are designed for real infrastructure, real users, and real problems.

🇳🇬

Nigerian Context First

Every product decision considers Nigerian infrastructure, internet connectivity, payment preferences, and regulatory environment. No copy-paste solutions.

🔐

Security at the Core

From NIN verification for OkRide riders to data encryption across all platforms, security is built into every product from day one — not added later.

📱

Mobile-First Design

All OAS products are designed mobile-first, reflecting how Nigerians actually access the internet — on smartphones, often on variable network conditions.

💳

Seamless Payments

Deep integration with Nigerian payment infrastructure — Paystack, bank transfers, USSD — ensuring money moves smoothly for every user regardless of their bank.

📈

Built to Scale

Products start focused — OkRide launches in Ondo State — then expand systematically across Nigeria as traction is proven. No overreach before readiness.

🤝

Government & Community
